Blanaid Hogan's Biography


Blanaid Hogan was born in Achill Sound, British Columbia in 1996. She grew up in Achill Sound
before moving to Kenmare, Massachusetts after high school. When she moved to Kenmare,
she started working for Kenmare Hospital School for Children (KHSC) and her life has been
chaotic ever since. She’s had an interest in web programming since high school and has tried to
learn from various guides, but only got as far as HTML and CSS. Then when seeing that the
KHSC’s website hadn’t been updated in years, she decided to take a web programming class
because she needed an excuse.
